Frequenty Asked Questions

I'm starving! How soon can I get my order?

Items marked as In Stock can be picked up or delivered the same day. For items marked as Available to Order we will coordinate with you after receiving your order form.

I have fallen and cannot get up! Do you deliver?

Yes! for a small flat fee of $10 we will deliver anywhere in London. If outside of London prices will vary.

How can I pay for my order?

Currently we accept cash and e-transfer. Card payments are coming soon!


I forgot I'm on a diet! How can I cancel the order?

To cancel an order please reach out to use to our email forestcitybakery@gmail.com

I live in a Tibetan monastery and heard about your magnificent treats, can you ship them to me?

Currently we only service London Ontario, but we will be expanding soon!

Is there a minimum order size?

Yes, for most items we have a minimum of one dozen per order, however, if you would like to purchase smaller quantities or try before you buy, we have a spot at Trail's End market on Saturdays from 7:30AM until 3:30PM
